## 🔍 About This Application

This AI-Powered Air Quality Command Center provides an easy way to monitor air pollution levels. It uses AI to analyze data and shows it with clear, real-time charts. The application runs on Windows and shows current pollution, past trends, and future forecasts. It is built on WPF and uses Syncfusion Charts to present data visually. This helps you understand the air quality around you and make smarter choices about your environment.

## 🖥️ System Requirements

Before you download, make sure your computer meets these requirements:

- Windows 10 or newer (64-bit recommended)
- At least 4 GB of RAM
- 500 MB of free disk space
- Internet connection for live data updates
- .NET Framework 4.7.2 or newer installed (usually pre-installed on Windows 10+)

## ⚙️ Features You Will Use

- **Real-Time Air Pollution Data:** See current air quality with easy-to-read charts.
- **Historical Trends:** Look back at pollution changes over days, weeks, or months.
- **AI-Based Forecasts:** Get predictions on air quality to plan ahead.
- **Interactive Visual Charts:** Explore data points using zoom and filter tools.
- **User-Friendly Interface:** No technical knowledge needed.
- **Syncfusion WPF Chart Integration:** Provides detailed and smooth graphing experience.
- **Busy Indicator:** Shows when data is loading to keep you informed.
- **Multiple Air Quality Indicators:** Includes PM2.5, PM10, CO, NO2, O3 levels, and more.

## 🔧 Using the Application

Once open, the main screen shows a map with air quality colors, graphs, and current conditions:

- **Map View:** Displays pollution spread in your city or selected area.
- **Charts:** You can switch between real-time data and past trends with tabs.
- **Forecast Section:** Shows air quality predictions for the next hours or days.
- **Refresh Button:** Click to reload data from the internet.
- **Settings:** Choose which air pollutants to display or the update frequency.

Use your mouse to hover over data points in charts. The values will appear in small pop-ups for precise reading.

If the app takes a moment to load data, a "busy indicator" will show. This means it is working in the background.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting

- **App does not start:**  
  Ensure your Windows has .NET Framework 4.7.2 or newer installed. Restart your PC and try again.

- **Charts are empty or no data:**  
  Check your internet connection. The app needs online access to fetch live air quality data.

- **Installation fails:**  
  Try running the installer as an administrator. Right-click the `.exe` file and select "Run as administrator."

- **App freezes during use:**  
  Close the app from the Task Manager and open it again. If the problem continues, check for updates.
